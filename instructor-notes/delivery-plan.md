# Instructor Delivery Plan

## Recommended flow

Use these labs after the 6-hour theory session. The theory gives the mental model; the labs turn it into working workflows.

## Guided labs

- Lab 00: Prerequisites
- Lab 01: First workflow
- Lab 02: Node.js CI
- Lab 03: Pull request checks
- Lab 08: Docker build and push
- Lab 10: Environments and approvals
- Lab 11: Troubleshooting

## Independent or paired labs

- Lab 04: Matrix strategy
- Lab 05: Cache
- Lab 06: Artifacts
- Lab 07: Docker build
- Lab 09: Manual deployment
- Lab 12: Final homework

## Suggested breakpoints

- Break after Lab 03 once students understand CI and PR feedback.
- Break after Lab 06 before moving into Docker.
- Break before Lab 11 so troubleshooting has enough energy and focus.

## Instructor guidance

- Keep students reading workflow logs, not guessing.
- Ask students to identify the failed job, failed step, command, and error.
- Keep real deployment as echo commands unless your class has a prepared target environment.
